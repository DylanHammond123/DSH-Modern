FAQs.

Everything you need to know before picking up the phone.

If your boiler is over 10–12 years old, frequently breaking down, making unusual noises or producing higher energy bills than usual, it's often more cost-effective to replace it. That said, Joby will always give you an honest assessment before recommending anything — he'll never push you towards a new boiler if a repair will do the job.

In most cases, a straightforward like-for-like boiler swap can be completed within a single day. If pipework alterations or a system change is required, it may take a day or two. Joby will give you a realistic timeframe before starting so you can plan accordingly.

Yes, absolutely. Joby is fully Gas Safe registered, which is a legal requirement for anyone working on gas appliances in the UK. You can ask to see his Gas Safe ID card at any time — he'll always have it on him. Never let anyone work on your gas appliances without checking this.

We recommend getting your boiler serviced once a year, ideally before winter sets in (September or October is perfect). Regular servicing keeps your boiler running efficiently, helps prevent breakdowns at the worst possible time, and is often a requirement to keep your manufacturer's warranty valid.
