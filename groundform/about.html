Our Story

Started with a Spade,
Built on Reputation

Groundform Landscapes was founded by Henry over a decade ago, born out of a genuine passion for the outdoors and a belief that every garden — whatever its size or condition — has the potential to be something special.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted local team, but the values have never changed: honest pricing, quality materials, meticulous workmanship, and treating every client's garden as if it were our own.

Over the years we've built hundreds of gardens across Colchester and Essex — from simple lawn and patio installs to full-scale garden transformations. Each one gets the same care and attention. Each one we're proud to put our name on.
